# EIR request: Newham parking/TMO schedule plans

Subject: Environmental Information Regulations request – Newham parking restriction GIS/TMO schedule plans

Dear London Borough of Newham,

Please treat this as a request under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004.

I am requesting the most recent digital data held by the Council, or by contractors/suppliers on the Council’s behalf, for on-street parking and waiting/loading restrictions in the London Borough of Newham.

Please provide the information in any available digital format, including but not limited to GIS, GeoJSON, Shapefile, GML, MapInfo, CAD, CSV, Excel, PDF schedule plans, Traffweb/Causeway exports, or other files held by contractors.

The request covers:

- Controlled Parking Zone boundaries and operating hours
- Resident permit bays
- Paid parking bays
- Free short-stay bays
- Limited waiting bays
- Disabled / blue badge bays
- Loading bays
- No loading restrictions
- Single yellow lines
- Double yellow lines
- No waiting / no stopping restrictions
- Red route / clearway restrictions where held by the Council
- Motorcycle bays, taxi bays, EV bays and other kerbside restriction types
- Side of road, road name, geometry/coordinates, restriction type, operating days/times, max stay, no return, tariff/payment status, permit zone and any linked Traffic Management Order identifiers

If exact GIS geometry is held by a contractor or map provider, please provide the exported files or confirm the contractor/provider and the file formats available.

If any part of the data is already published, please provide the direct download links or ArcGIS REST service URLs rather than only a webpage link.

I would prefer machine-readable formats such as GeoJSON, Shapefile, CSV or Excel. If these are not available, please provide PDFs or schedule plans.

Yours faithfully,
[Your name]

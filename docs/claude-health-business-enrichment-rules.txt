HiStreets health-business enrichment rule

For HiStreets, do not prioritise or enrich large NHS hospitals, A&E departments, emergency departments, NHS Trust hospital sites or hospital departments.

Include local/private/NHS-contracted health businesses because they operate like local services and may be claimed/updated by owners or managers:
- pharmacy
- chemist
- dentist / dental practice
- optician / optical shop
- GP practice
- medical centre / medical center
- private clinic
- NHS-contracted clinic
- physiotherapy
- therapy clinic
- hearing clinic
- aesthetic clinic
- care/service clinic

Do not use Google Maps data. Use only legal public sources:
- official business website
- NHS service page
- CQC page where relevant
- official public social page
- owner confirmation
- shop-window evidence
- phone-call confirmation

For every enriched row, keep the original business_id. Return only rows where at least one of these fields was found from a legal source:
phone, website, whatsapp, email, opening_hours.

Every enriched row must include:
business_id,business_name,phone,website,whatsapp,email,opening_hours,source_type,evidence_note,source_url,confidence

Leave unknown fields blank. Do not guess.

HiStreets Privacy Policy

HiStreets is a free Newham community platform. We collect the minimum data needed to run accounts, business verification, saved places, posts and reports.

What we collect

For users: email address and display name. For verified businesses: public business contact details, verification outcome and public posts. We do not store live location tracking.

Why we use it

Account data is used to provide the service. Business verification and moderation are used for trust and safety. Notifications are opt-in consent only.

Where it is stored

Data is stored in Supabase. The operator must create the Supabase project in a UK/EU region.

Your rights

From Profile you can download your data and delete your account. Deletion removes your profile and saved places and anonymises your posts/reports.

Documents

Verification documents, if used, are private and must be deleted within 30 days after review. HiStreets stores only the review outcome.

Contact

Contact the HiStreets operator for privacy requests.

HiStreets Terms

HiStreets is a free community platform for Newham. By using it, you agree to use it lawfully and respectfully.

Age

The platform is intended for people aged 16+. Youth job listings are informational and must include a real application route.

Posts

Offers, jobs, free meals and community posts are reviewed before publication. Scam, illegal, discriminatory, misleading or unsafe content is not allowed.

Business verification

Verified badges are granted only after phone/document checks and human approval.

Parking

Parking layers show official or verified data only. Always check roadside signs, bay plates and road markings before parking.

No monetisation

HiStreets is operated as a free community platform with no ads, payments or tracking SDKs.

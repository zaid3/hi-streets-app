HiStreets Privacy Policy

HiStreets is a free local platform for Newham. We collect only the information needed to run business accounts, business posts, job applications and moderation.

What we collect

For businesses, we collect login email, business details, public posts and verification status. For job applicants, we collect the details they choose to submit for a job application, including their CV. For visitors, location is optional and is used to show nearby results.

Location

If you allow location, your browser shares it with the app so nearby offers, jobs and local places can be shown. HiStreets does not store live location tracking.

Why we use it

We use this information to show local offers, jobs and community posts, manage business access, moderate content and send job applications to the relevant business.

What we do not do

We do not sell personal data. We do not use advertising trackers. We do not use your data for unrelated marketing.

Where it is stored

Data is stored in Supabase. The operator should keep the Supabase project in a UK/EU region.

Your rights

You can request access to your data or ask for your account to be deleted. Businesses can delete their account from the Business area where this feature is available.

Documents and CVs

Verification documents and CVs are private. CVs are shared only with the business connected to the job application and authorised admins for support and moderation.

Contact

Contact the HiStreets operator for privacy requests.
